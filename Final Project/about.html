WHO ARE THE DUDE-O's?

Seth Gundlach AKA IronApe98 enjoys having fun while gaming, but also tends to have a more strategic approach to gaming. He plays single player story games and enjoys some third person shooters. IronApe98 also occasionally enjoys a good racing game.

Joaquin Wahlmeier AKA Walkamolie22 now brings his cooky playstyle and sarcasm to DUDE-O's. He plays a range of games consisting of first person and third person shooters. You can find a bit of nostalgia here as He enjoys playing older classics as well.

The History of DUDE-O's: DUDE-O's was founded by Seth Gundlach and Micah Jones. At the time, the two were playing a lot of duos in Fortnite. On August 2, 2023, Micah and Seth logged in to play another match and both had the "Dude" skin.

Micah texted Seth, "I see you got Dude. You and I are now....#DudeSquad." Later that day, Seth replied, "Just duos. #dudeos."

After coming up with the name, the two decided to finally pull the trigger on their idea. Unfortunately, Micah felt like he had too much on his plate and had to step away. After doing some gaming with his friend Joaquin, Seth decided to ask if he was interested in co-running the Dude-O's YouTube channel. Joaquin agreed and on November 14, 2023 that pair launched the Dude-O's YouTube channel.
